Case · B2B HR · ~3 weeks

Personal profile + company page: measurable lift in three weeks

Built from meeting notes across four calls (17 March → 7 April). I do not publish the client’s name or agency brand on the site—cleaner for NDAs and reputation; happy to confirm context in DM if needed.

Context

Who: founder of an HR agency focused on leadership and hard-to-fill roles.

Starting point: the profile existed but headline and About did not carry the value proposition; almost no reactions on posts; starting SSI 33/100; 291 connections; no clear content spine or “intro” posts aligned to goals.

Numbers over the period (personal profile)

33 → 45 SSI (+12 points, ~+36% vs start)
291 → 457 connections (+166, ~+57%)
→ 455 followers by the fourth checkpoint (network + visibility)
0–1 → 7 & 5 reactions on first posts after the refresh—early sign distribution picked up
657 post impressions in 7 days at the checkpoint (+5.7% vs prior window)
2,201 search appearances for the profile (reported slice)

Company page on LinkedIn

In parallel we launched the company page: naming, URL, visuals, description, contacts, and first posts that explain the agency and bridge to the founder’s profile.

In a short window the page moved from zero to a working rhythm: 26 followers at the checkpoint, invitations into the company community, agreed posting cadence, and themes tied to live sessions for an HR audience.

What we did across sessions (short)

  1. Kickoff: SSI and profile audit, headline and About, skills and education, content pillars, intro posts, relevant outbound connections and comments on target accounts.
  2. Second call: company page build, founder ↔ company content split, plan and posts from both sides.
  3. Third: scheduled posts for the week, community invites, LinkedIn limits, webinar themes.
  4. Fourth: three-week readout, personal and page metrics, webinar prep, invites to the company space.

Not a promise everyone gets the same figures—niche, baseline, and your work between calls always differ. It does show what packaging + cadence + network + a company page can unlock together.
