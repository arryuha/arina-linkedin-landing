Problem

We align the work with your case—projects, a job change, public presence, grants, and more. Below: the top three asks, what I call success in 2–3 months, and what often blocks people without a partner.

Top 3: why people show up on LinkedIn

  • Target connections—who matters in your network, how to start conversations, how to reach “your” people instead of random invites
  • Investors / trust—a public line and signals you can stand behind—no fairy-tale “guaranteed deal”
  • Personal brand—positioning, voice, differentiation, topic anchors—something you own, not a copy of everyone else’s feed

Also common: project leads, a job move, grants, general visibility—if that is the goal, we build the plan around it.

What success means: 2–3 months in

For me the sign it is working: steady regularity (not “loud for a week, then gone”), clear positioning so the right people read you in the right frame, and a system tied to the goal—columns, cadence, networking, metrics you can live with without an empty feed.

What usually gets in the way

  • “I have nothing to say” and a hollow or generic profile
  • two good weeks, then you vanish from the feed
  • it feels awkward to be public—you cannot name your edge
  • “done for you” services without depth—someone else’s voice
  • no time to run the platform, comments, and network on your own

FAQ

Is this LinkedIn SMM? I feel like I have nothing to say.

No—it is not “post for the sake of posting.” The product is ongoing guidance: you learn to use LinkedIn as a tool, and I stay in the process with copy, structure, and networking. “It is all the same as everyone else” is a normal starting point. From there we pull out your themes, angles, and columns—so there is a reason to post and a voice that fits you.

How much time does this take?

Calls are usually about an hour. Between calls you still need time for copy, posts, comments, and messages—the load depends on the goal and the cadence we agree. Up front we estimate what you can realistically do per week and shape the plan around that.

Do you write posts for me?

I can help with structure, drafts, and edits, but you publish and own the meaning—that is safer for reputation and for legal guardrails. If you want something close to ghostwriting, we discuss it explicitly and only where it is ethical and allowed.

Employer rules, NDAs, no public case studies

Common situation. We set constraints early: what can be named, how, what phrasing to avoid. Content is built so it does not break company policy or confidentiality agreements.

Do you guarantee leads or reach?

No honest guarantees on numbers—too many variables. I do guarantee a process: clear steps, feedback, and adjustments based on what we see in the profile and activity. If inbound is the goal, we frame hypotheses and test them together.

What happens if I message you on Telegram?

Short version: goal, context, time limits, plus the admin and payment pieces so the start is clean for both sides. Then 1–2 opening one-hour interviews to unpack your personal brand and gather material for the profile and content. After that—regular calls and Telegram between them.

Which language should I use on LinkedIn?

Depends on the audience—often English, sometimes Russian, sometimes a split strategy. We pick one primary language for posts and the headline first so the signal stays sharp.

I am a developer using LinkedIn to land new offers—is this for me?

Yes. We tilt the work toward recruiters and hiring managers: profile and activity that read clearly, sustainable cadence, careful networking and DMs, content about stack and real work without breaking NDAs. I do not promise an offer—too many market variables—but we can build a clear path: you stay visible, conversations happen, you do not vanish between applications.

Do you run HR brand LinkedIn groups for executives / C-level?

Yes—HR brand and LinkedIn group programs where leaders and senior executives participate. That is a separate line of work: content, cadence, engagement, alignment with HR goals. It usually involves more approvals and a different scope than 1:1 retainer. Message on Telegram with your company context and I will propose a shape.
